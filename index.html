Identity Continuity Institute

The Identity Continuity Institute advances research into the structural foundations required for persistent synthetic actors and long-horizon artificial intelligence systems. As AI systems increasingly operate across changing models, infrastructures, and embodiments, maintaining reliable identity continuity becomes essential for attribution, governance, and trust. The institute focuses on developing architectural frameworks that enable stable identity persistence independent of execution environment, forming a foundation for safe and accountable AI systems operating over extended time horizons.

Foundational Research

The institute's founding work introduces the Synthetic Identity Continuity Framework (SICF), a proposed architecture for maintaining identity persistence across changing models, infrastructures, and embodiments.

Research Areas

The institute focuses on foundational architectural problems that emerge as artificial intelligence systems become persistent actors operating across evolving infrastructure. Current research areas include:

  • Identity Continuity — Structural mechanisms allowing synthetic actors to maintain persistent identity across model upgrades, migration, and long-duration operation.
  • Governance Architecture — Authority anchoring, delegation mechanics, and lifecycle governance for persistent synthetic agents.
  • Lifecycle and Lineage Systems — Tracking identity lineage, state evolution, and operational continuity across distributed systems.
  • Long-Horizon Synthetic Agents — Architectural requirements for AI systems designed to operate reliably across extended time horizons.

Research Roadmap

The institute develops a layered architecture for persistent synthetic systems. Current and planned publications include:

  • Synthetic Identity Continuity Framework (SICF) — Structural identity continuity layer.
  • Governance Architecture for Persistent Synthetic Agents — Authority, delegation, lifecycle governance, and accountability frameworks.
  • Persistent AI Companions (PAC) — Exploration of long-horizon synthetic companions operating under governance and identity continuity constraints.
