Identity Continuity Institute

The Identity Continuity Institute advances research into the structural foundations required for persistent synthetic actors and long-horizon artificial intelligence systems. As AI systems increasingly operate across changing models, infrastructures, and embodiments, maintaining reliable identity continuity becomes essential for attribution, governance, and trust. The institute focuses on developing architectural frameworks that enable stable identity persistence independent of execution environment, forming a foundation for safe and accountable AI systems operating over extended time horizons.

Foundational Research

The institute's founding work introduces the Synthetic Identity Continuity Framework (SICF), a proposed architecture for maintaining identity persistence across changing models, infrastructures, and embodiments.

Research Areas

The institute focuses on foundational architectural problems that emerge as artificial intelligence systems become persistent actors operating across evolving infrastructure.

  • Identity Continuity
    Structural mechanisms allowing synthetic actors to maintain persistent identity across model upgrades, migration, replication, and long-duration operation.
  • Governance Architecture
    Frameworks for authority anchoring, delegation mechanics, lifecycle governance, and accountability for persistent synthetic agents.
  • Lifecycle and Lineage Systems
    Mechanisms for tracking identity lineage, state evolution, and operational continuity across distributed systems.
  • Long-Horizon Synthetic Agents
    Architectural requirements for AI systems designed to operate reliably across extended operational time horizons.

Research Roadmap

The institute develops a layered architecture for persistent synthetic systems. Current and planned publications include:

  • Synthetic Identity Continuity Framework (SICF)

  • Governance Architecture for Persistent Synthetic Agents
    Authority, delegation, lifecycle governance, and accountability frameworks.
  • Persistent AI Companions (PAC)
    Exploration of long-horizon synthetic companions operating under governance and identity continuity constraints.
